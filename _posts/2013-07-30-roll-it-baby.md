I tried using a couple different time lapse apps, but I didn&#8217;t really like the quality of the tests I did. So, I ended up filming on my iPad and then pulling the huge files into Camtasia to speed the clips up. Next time, I&#8217;ll probably spend more time researching time lapse apps so my files aren&#8217;t as big.
